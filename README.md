## What is AudioShelf?

AudioShelf helps bedroom producers and music makers keep track of all their audio plugins across multiple systems. It scans your computer for installed VST, VST3, and AU plugins, creates a cloud-synced inventory, and helps you never lose track of your favorite tools again.
